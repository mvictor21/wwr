Purpose

[The purpose of this website, is to demonstrate the habilities gained by studying on BYU-Idaho online and being part of the group WDD 130 - Web Fundamentals. Since other students will be part of this course in the future, this website can inspire them to continue and move forward, facing their own difficulties. This website, will benefit me as a personal inspiration that I can do anything with the help of my Heavenly Father and my Saviour]

Audience

[This website is for those who feels uncomfortable the first weeks of the Web Fundamentals course, this website will show how they can trust in Heavenly Father's help through any circumstances of their lifes. With a simplicity of colors and messages that will make them reflect on what's the most important when studying for the future]
